Know more about us...

Veggie Plan is a free app dedicated to our vegetarian community. Has more than 500 recipes, which contain main courses, snacks and beverages. Each includes photos and also vegan, gluten-free or whole grain options.

Our goal is to encourage people to go vegeterian and understand that you can live a cruelty free life, saving thousands of animals a year eating delicious meals.
